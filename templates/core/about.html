I Am That Woman

Who we are & Our Mission?

I Am That Woman is an organization dedicated to empowering marginalized women by providing them with ideas, skills, mentorship, and opportunities. Our mission is to create a safe space for women to grow, learn, and thrive in their personal and professional lives.

Through workshops, mentorship, seminars, and conferences, we help all who identify as women to find their voice and grow in their professional and personal lives. We believe in creating opportunities for women to network, connect, and support each other in their journey towards success.

We understand that the progression of growth, from the mentorship to the actual implementation of skills, can be challenging. That's why we provide hands-on training, ongoing support, and a community of like-minded women who are ready to lift each other up.

Join us as we continue building a future where every woman has access to the resources, education, and network she needs to succeed. Together, we are creating a legacy of empowered women who empower others.
